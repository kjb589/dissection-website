    It's tough to live without a pair of noise cancelling headphones these days. They are almost necessary to get through commercial flights, workouts, and commutes via public transportation. Noise cancelling technology has been around for longer than most people realize, with the first ever pair of noise cancelling headphones being created by Bose® founder Dr. Amar Bose in 1978.
    KVIDIO's WH304B model is an affordable pair of headphones offering comfort, versatility, and disturbance-free listening. The sound quality of these headphones could not compete against more popular, expensive brands like Bose®, Apple, or Sony, but for $20, they have a lot to offer.
    KVIDIO is the sister brand of BERIBES which offers a near identical pair in the WH305A. Both headphones can be found at Amazon, and both brands have storefronts there as well. View KVIDIO products on Amazon.
